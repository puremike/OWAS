What is an English Auction?

An English auction is the most popular and recognizable auction type. Bidding starts at a low opening price and increases as participants place higher bids. The auction ends when no higher bids are placed, and the highest bidder wins the item.

How It Works:

  • Auctioneer starts with a base price.
  • Bidders openly place incrementally higher bids.
  • When no more bids are made, the highest bidder wins.

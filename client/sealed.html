What is a Sealed Bid Auction?

In a Sealed Bid Auction, all bidders submit their bids confidentially without knowing the offers of other participants. Once all bids are received, the seller reviews them and awards the item to the highest bidder. This format prevents bidding wars but increases strategic decision-making.

How It Works:

  • Participants submit a single, confidential bid.
  • No bidder knows how much others are offering.
  • At a set deadline, the highest bid is revealed and wins.

Example Scenario:

A government agency is auctioning off a piece of land. Multiple developers submit sealed bids by a deadline. After all bids are opened, the highest bidder (Developer A with $2 million) wins the auction without knowing what others bid beforehand.
